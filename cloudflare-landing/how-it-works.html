Frequently Asked Questions

Is GreenChainz free to use?

For buyers (architects, contractors), GreenChainz is 100% free. Suppliers can list up to 10 products for free. Paid tiers unlock unlimited listings and advanced features.

How do you verify sustainability claims?

We cross-reference all certifications (FSC, LEED, B Corp) with official databases. Every product displays its verification status. Learn more about our verification process.

Can I request quotes for custom quantities?

Yes! Use our RFQ system to request quotes for any quantity. Suppliers typically respond within 48 hours.

Do you ship internationally?

Shipping depends on the supplier. Each product page shows the supplier's service areas. Use location filters to find local suppliers.

How do I become a verified supplier?

Sign up here. Our team will guide you through the verification process (typically 2-3 business days).
