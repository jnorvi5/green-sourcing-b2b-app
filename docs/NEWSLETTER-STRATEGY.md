# The Green Spec: Newsletter Strategy & Execution Plan

**Objective:** Build a pre-launch waitlist of 500+ architects and sustainability professionals for GreenChainz.
**Format:** Bi-weekly Email Newsletter.
**Name:** *The Green Spec* (Sustainability + Specification).
**Tagline:** "The bi-weekly intelligence briefing for sustainable specification."

---

## 1. Content Strategy & Pillars

The content is designed to provide high value upfront, positioning GreenChainz as an authority before the platform even launches.

### Core Pillars
1.  **Industry News (The Radar):** Regulatory updates, LEED v5 changes, local carbon mandates (e.g., NY LL97, CA Title 24).
2.  **Material Spotlight (Deep Dive):** Technical breakdown of one green material (performance, cost, carbon data).
3.  **How-To Guides (Practical):** Actionable workflows for architects (e.g., "How to calculate embodied carbon").
4.  **Supplier Profiles (The Source):** Interviews with founding suppliers to humanize the supply chain.
5.  **GreenChainz Updates (The Build):** Transparency on platform progress, new features, and beta access.

**Call to Action (CTA):** "Join the beta waitlist to access these tools first."
