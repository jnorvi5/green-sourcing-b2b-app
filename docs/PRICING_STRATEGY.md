**Takeaway:** The proposed GreenChainz pricing ($0 / $199 / $499) is **highly competitive**.

*   The **Premium tier ($499/mo)** undercuts 2050 Materials' top tier ($540/mo) and is significantly cheaper than Alibaba's premium options, while offering high-touch service (certification fast-track).

## 2. Value Proposition Valuation

Why will suppliers pay?

1.  **Verified Data (Trust):** In a market flooded with greenwashing, the "Verified by GreenChainz" badge acts like a digital passport. Competitors like Alibaba charge ~$22k/year for their top "Verified Supplier" tier. We offer this trust signal at a fraction of the cost in our Pro/Premium tiers.
2.  **Architect RFQs (Leads):** A single commercial project specification can be worth $50k-$500k. Paying $199/mo is trivial if it generates even *one* qualified lead per year. Alibaba limits RFQ responses (20/mo for Basic); we can offer "unlimited" or high-volume RFQ access to disrupt.
3.  **Analytics (Intelligence):** Manufacturers are blind. They don't know who is looking at their products. 2050 Materials gates this behind their €6k/yr tier. Offering basic analytics in Pro and advanced in Premium is a massive differentiator.

## 3. Recommended 3-Tier Pricing Model

### **Tier 1: Starter (Free)**
*   **Target:** Small manufacturers, skepticism reduction, "land and expand".
*   **Price:** **$0/month**
*   **Features:**
    *   **5 Product Listings:** Enough to showcase a core range, but not a full catalog.
    *   **Basic Profile:** Company logo, description, website link.
    *   **Passive Visibility:** Products appear in search results.
    *   **Limited RFQs:** Receive RFQs but must upgrade to *respond* (or view full contact details).
*   **Justification:** Removes barrier to entry. Populates the marketplace with inventory (solving the chicken-and-egg problem).

### **Tier 2: Pro (Growth)**
*   **Target:** SMEs, active sellers who need leads.
*   **Price:** **$199/month** (or $1,990/year - 2 months free)
*   **Features:**
    *   **Unlimited Listings:** Upload full catalog.
    *   **Verified Badge:** Visual trust signal on all products (after audit).
    *   **Full RFQ Access:** Receive, view, and respond to unlimited RFQs.
    *   **Basic Analytics:** "Your product appeared in 50 searches this month."
    *   **RFQ Notifications:** Instant email alerts.
*   **Justification:** $199 is the industry standard "sweet spot" (Alibaba, 2050 Materials). It acts as a qualifying filter for serious suppliers without being prohibitive.

### **Tier 3: Premium (Enterprise)**
*   **Target:** Large manufacturers, certification-heavy brands.
*   **Price:** **$499/month** (or $4,990/year)
*   **Features:**
    *   **Everything in Pro.**
    *   **Featured Placement:** Top of search results for category keywords.
    *   **Advanced Analytics:** "Architects from [Firm Name] viewed your insulation."
    *   **Concierge Onboarding:** We upload their data for them (huge pain point).
    *   **Certification Fast-Track:** Priority verification queue.
    *   **API Access:** Sync inventory directly.
*   **Justification:** Comparable to 2050 Materials' €6k/yr tier. Large companies will pay for visibility and data services that save their sales team time.

## 5. Implementation Strategy
1.  **Launch with "Founding Member" Discount:** Offer the first 50 suppliers "Pro for Life at $99/mo" to secure early liquidity.
2.  **Gate the RFQ Response:** This is the strongest lever. Free users see "You have a new lead from a LEED Gold project in NY" but must upgrade to unlock it.
3.  **Manual Verification First:** Do not build automated billing yet. Invoice manually via Stripe/Quickbooks for the first 10 customers to validate willingness to pay.
