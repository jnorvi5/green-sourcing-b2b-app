# GreenChainz B2B Marketplace Business Plan

**Prepared:** November 2025  
**Company:** GreenChainz LLC  
**Mission:** Global Trust Layer for Sustainable Commerce

## Executive Summary

GreenChainz is a B2B SaaS marketplace connecting architects, contractors, and real estate developers with verified suppliers of sustainable building materials. Our product features a searchable, standardized catalog of eco-certified products, integrated RFQ workflow, verification via blockchain (planned feature), and a growing supplier network. Our primary revenue will be SaaS subscriptions from suppliers, transaction commissions, and data API access.

**Key Highlights:**
- **Market Size:** $483.7B global market (2025), growing to $1.4T by 2034 (12.5% CAGR)
- **Break-Even:** Month 6-7 (15-20 paying suppliers at $280/mo average)
- **Year 1 MRR Target:** $16,240 (40 Pro + 18 Premium suppliers)
- **Gross Margin:** 82% (SaaS economics)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 3.2:1 (healthy unit economics)

## Products & Services

### Core Platform Features

#### 1. Marketplace Platform (Architects/Buyers)
- **Search & Filter:** By material type, certifications, carbon footprint, price, lead time
- **Product Comparison:** Side-by-side comparison with standardized eco-metrics
- **RFQ System:** Send requests for quotes directly to multiple suppliers
- **Project Management:** Save materials, track RFQ status, analytics
- **Verification:** Real-time certification checking via data provider APIs

#### 2. Supplier Dashboard
- **Product Upload:** Bulk CSV import, individual product pages
- **Analytics:** Views, RFQ response rates, conversion tracking, leaderboard rank

#### 3. Data Provider Integration
- **API Connectors:** FSC, B Corp, Building Transparency (EC3), LEED, Cradle to Cradle

- **Message Board:** 5 channels (General, Architect Corner, Supplier Announcements, Expert Q&A, Standardization)

- **Events:** Webinars, virtual trade shows, group certification workshops

## Business Model

| Tier | Price | Features | Target Audience |

#### 2. Transaction Fees (Phase 2 - Month 9+)

- **Payment Processing:** Platform facilitates payments, earns processing margin
- **Estimate (Year 1):** $2,000 from pilot transactions

#### 3. Data & API Licensing (Enterprise)

- **Bulk Data Access:** CSV exports, analytics dashboards

#### 4. Advertising & Sponsorships (Future)

- **Banner Ads:** Targeted to architect/contractor audience
