# GreenChainz Product Overview

GreenChainz is a B2B green sourcing marketplace connecting sustainability-minded buyers (architects, contractors, procurement teams) with verified green suppliers.

## Core Value Proposition

**Data Moat**: Verifiable sustainability data aggregated from multiple sources (EPDs, certifications, carbon footprints) presented in a single, trusted platform.

## Target Users

**Buyers**: Architects, contractors, and procurement teams seeking to discover, compare, and de-risk sourcing of green materials with "apples-to-apples" comparisons.

**Suppliers**: Regional materials manufacturers looking to get sustainable products in front of qualified buyers without high marketing costs.

## Key Features

- Product search and filtering by sustainability metrics
- EPD (Environmental Product Declaration) aggregation and verification
- Certification verification (LEED, BREEAM, FSC, etc.)
- Carbon footprint calculations and comparisons
- RFQ (Request for Quote) system
- Supplier/buyer dashboards
- Multi-source data integration (EPD International, WAP Sustainability, Building Transparency, Autodesk, FSC)

## Compliance Standards

- ISO 14025: Type III Environmental Declarations (EPDs)
- EN 15804: Sustainability of construction works
- ISO 21930: Environmental declarations for building products
- EPD Hub GPI v1.3: General Program Instructions for EPD verification

## Current Status

MVP development phase focusing on data provider partnerships, supplier onboarding, and core marketplace functionality.
